# Lesson 1: Introduction to Text Analytics

## Duration
2 hours

---

## Learning Objectives
By the end of this lesson, students will be able to:
1. Understand how text can be analysed computationally
2. Identify common text analytics use cases
3. Recognise limitations and bias in text-based systems

---

## Lesson Flow

### 1. Icebreaker (15 mins)
Students list places where text data appears:
- Reviews
- Feedback
- Emails
- Chat messages

---

### 2. Concept Introduction (30 mins)
- What makes text different from numbers?
- Why text needs pre-processing
- Simple idea of turning words into numbers

---

### 3. Case Study (35 mins)
Example: Customer feedback analysis
Students discuss:
- What insights can be extracted?
- What context might be lost?

---

### 4. Bias & Limitations Discussion (25 mins)
- Language ambiguity
- Cultural context
- Misclassification risks

---

### 5. Reflection (15 mins)
Students answer:
> “Why might a text analytics system misunderstand people?”

---

## Assessment (Formative)
- Participation
- Thoughtfulness of reflection
